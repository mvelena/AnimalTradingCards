Lion

The only cats that live in groups are lions.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera leo
  • Diet: Carnivore
  • Type: Mammals
  • Weight: 265 to 420 pounds

A lion is a big and very beautiful cat. Lions live in a group, pride. There can be up to 30 lions in a pride based on the availability of water and food. The main hunters are female lions. Lions sleep a lot, 16-20 hours every day. Usually, a lion has 1-4 cubs.
